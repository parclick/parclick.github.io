Technical Lead Architect in Madrid (we are hiring!)

Who we are

Parclick.com is an online marketplace for parking reservations.

We offer high discounts at affiliated car parkings. We deal with the whole online booking process and customer support. In the end, our customer is happy because of the discounts and our affiliated car parkings get additional revenue.

We are growing fast and are positioning Parclick in European markets such as Spain and Portugal, France, Italy and more countries to come.

We are currently based in Madrid and working globally.

Our Team
